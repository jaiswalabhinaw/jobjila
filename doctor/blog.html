Tips, Exercises & Guidance for Healthy Eyes

10 Tips for Daily Eye Hygiene

  • Wash your hands before touching your eyes or lenses.
  • Never rub your eyes aggressively; it can damage the cornea.
  • Clean your glasses or lenses regularly with proper solution.
  • Avoid sharing eye makeup or lenses with others.
  • Use protective glasses when outdoors or in dusty environments.
  • Keep digital screens clean to reduce glare and strain.
  • Remove makeup completely before sleeping.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your eyes moist and healthy.
  • Use lubricating drops if you often feel dryness.
  • Visit an eye doctor once a year for preventive check-ups.

Special Disease Care Tips

  • For Glaucoma: Take prescribed drops regularly and avoid skipping doses.
  • For Diabetic Retinopathy: Maintain blood sugar levels and attend routine eye exams.
  • For Dry Eye Syndrome: Use artificial tears, avoid prolonged screen use, and stay hydrated.
  • For Cataract Patients: Protect eyes from UV rays and follow post-surgery care strictly.
  • For Macular Degeneration: Eat leafy greens, avoid smoking, and use low-vision aids if prescribed.
