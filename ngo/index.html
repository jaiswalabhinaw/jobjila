About Us

At Shatakshi NGO, we believe every life deserves dignity, compassion, and hope. From providing free last rites to delivering education, healthcare, and meals, our journey is built on selfless service and community support.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families through education, healthcare, and dignified end-of-life support.

Our Vision

A society where no one is left behind — where dignity, compassion, and opportunity are for all.

“Service to humanity is the rent we pay for our room on Earth. We are the hands that give dignity to the forgotten, a final farewell to those left alone, and hope to the community in its darkest hours. Our work is a testament to the belief that every life, no matter how quiet its end, deserves respect and remembrance.”

Our Philosophy

At Shatakshi Seva Sansthan, our philosophy rests on the belief that dignity is a universal right — one that extends beyond life into death. Guided by the principle of “Seva Parmo Dharma” (Service is our highest religion), this is not just a motto, but the cornerstone of everything we do.

We step in where society often looks away — with the unclaimed, the vulnerable, and the forgotten. By performing proper last rites for unclaimed bodies, we restore a fundamental piece of humanity. This act of compassion is our truest statement: that every life has value, and every individual, regardless of circumstances, deserves honor and remembrance.

Yet our philosophy goes beyond the final journey. We are equally committed to serving the living — responding to emergencies, feeding the hungry, educating children, and supporting the elderly. From offering free education and meals to extending a helping hand during crises, our goal is to act as a safety net and a source of hope.

For us, service is a continuous act of faith in humanity. It is the belief that through selfless action, we can create a society where every person feels seen, valued, and cared for.
